How do I place an order?

First, go to the menu page. Then, find the item you want to order. You can look at different items like salads and appetizers by clicking on the categories listed. When you find the item you want to order, click on its add to cart button. Next, click on the cart button in the upper right corner. You can review your order and change the amount of each item you want to order. Once you're ready to checkout, click on the checkout button on that pop up. The checkout will prompt you fill in your first name, last name, email, order specifications (if you have any), and form of payment. Once you have filled out all that info, click the place order button. You will be taken to an order confirmation page will give you a time estimate of when your order will be ready and your order number.

How do I get my food when it's ready?

When you place an order, we will give you a time estimate of when it will be ready. Around that time, head to Jack McGee's Pub, which is located on the bottom floor of Smith Union. Go to the cashier and tell them your name, order, and order number. They will charge you for your order and then give you your food.

I made a mistake in my order. How can I update my order?

Call the pub at 207-725-3888 and tell them your name, order, and order number. Then, tell them what you want your order to be updated to. Note: when you pick up your completed order, you will pay for the price of this updated order (not the wrong order you placed on ePub).
